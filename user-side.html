ORDER

  • HKS

If the user clicks on “Order”, Customers can view their orders in this page. At first state, customer’s orders are in processing State. For the second State, Admin will check with their payment by calling with phone and if the customer completed for the payment, customer’s orders reached in Success State. Success means that customers order is ready to deliver to their place. Otherwise, customer’s orders rejected by admin. Reject means that there is one of product that you ordered is unavailable.
